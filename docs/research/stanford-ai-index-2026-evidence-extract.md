## Positioning summary (one paragraph)

The AI Index 2026 quantifies the four forces that make a routing/abstraction layer the highest-leverage portfolio bet: (a) capability is accelerating *and* jagged (T1, T4) — point integrations rot, abstractions compound; (b) the frontier is flat across vendors (T2) and the supply chain is one fab deep (T3) — single-vendor lock-in is now a liability, not a shortcut; (c) incidents are growing 55% YoY (T6) — centralized audit is no longer optional; (d) sovereignty pressure is national-policy-level (T14). mcp-gateway is the surface on which the rest of the portfolio (airlok, Botnaut, Davis, TurboQuant, CRAM-Less, ELT) collects these tailwinds.
